# US Commission on International Religious Freedom (us-commission-on-international-religious-freedom)
The US Commission on International Religious Freedom (USCIRF) is an independent, bipartisan federal government commission that monitors religious freedom violations abroad and makes policy recommendations to the President, Secretary of State, and Congress.
